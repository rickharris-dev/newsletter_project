This is SoLife

Ever want to share some important life news with your social circles quickly? SoLife enables you to share thoughts, recommendations, and news with family and friends using LifeNotes, photos, and links. SoLife is not just for sharing what is on your mind, tell others what’s happening right now or about events coming up this weekend.

SoLife also connects businesses and consumers like never before. The marketplace let’s you know about deals, specials and coupons for businesses within your area. Tell SoLife the types of offers you are interested in, and you will be alerted of new deals in your area. Read reviews from your friends about the area businesses as well as reviews from those who live and work around you.

Are friends talking about the amazing concert they went to last night that you didn’t know was happening? Community events are central to the SoLife experience. Check the community calendar for the latest event information. Choose the distance you are willing to travel for an event and the category of events you are interested in. SoLife will keep you informed.

SoLife aims to augment life by bridging the everyday and the digital world. You control the information from friends that you see. This is your life. This is SoLife.
